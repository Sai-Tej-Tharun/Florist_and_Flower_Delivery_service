About This Service

Florals That Tell Your Love Story

Your wedding day deserves flowers as extraordinary as your love story. Our dedicated wedding floristry team works exclusively with couples to translate a vision — a mood board, a Pinterest board, or simply a feeling — into breathtaking floral reality.

We handle every floral element of your wedding: the bridal bouquet, bridesmaids' posies, boutonnieres, ceremony arch, aisle petals, table centrepieces, mantle arrangements, floral chandeliers, and reception décor. Each element is designed as part of a cohesive theme.

"Every wedding we design starts with one question: how do you want it to feel?"

— Shirin Patel, Lead Wedding Florist, Rose & Stem

Our Design Process

We begin with a complimentary 60-minute design consultation — in person or via video call — where we discuss your theme, venue, colour palette, guest count, and budget. Our florists then present a detailed mood board with flower suggestions, arrangement styles, and an itemised quote.

Book Early: Wedding floristry slots fill 3–6 months in advance, especially for peak season (October–February). We recommend enquiring as early as possible to secure your date.

What We Design

From a single bridal bouquet to full venue transformation — we scale to any wedding size. Our florals have graced intimate courthouse ceremonies of 20 guests and lavish 500-person palace receptions. No wedding is too intimate or too grand for our team.

Our Process

How We Bring Your Vision to Life

A seamless journey from first conversation to your wedding day — we handle every floral detail.

1

Free Design Consultation

60 minutes with your dedicated wedding florist — in person or via video call. We explore your theme, venue, palette, and budget.

2

Mood Board & Quote

Within 48 hours, we present a detailed mood board with flower choices, arrangement styles, and a full itemised quote.

3

Refinement & Booking

We refine the proposal to your feedback, finalise the floral list, and lock your date with a 30% booking deposit.

4

Venue Visit & Final Prep

We visit your venue to plan logistics, measure arches, and confirm centrepiece heights. Flowers are sourced and conditioned 3 days before.

5

Wedding Day Setup

Our team arrives early to install every element — from the ceremony arch to the last boutonniere — so your day starts perfectly.
