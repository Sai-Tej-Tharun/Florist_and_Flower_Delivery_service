About This Service

What Makes Our Bouquets Special?

At Rose & Stem, a bouquet is never just flowers — it's a handwritten letter in the language of petals. Our master florists begin each morning at 5 AM, selecting the finest stems from our network of certified sustainable farms across Karnataka, Maharashtra, and the Nilgiris.

From velvety red garden roses to blush peonies, spiky proteas, and whisper-soft gypsophila — every arrangement is designed to hold its beauty for 7–10 days with proper care. We wrap with triple-layer craft paper, tie with hand-dyed satin ribbon, and finish with your personalised message card.

"A single well-chosen bloom says more than a thousand carefully chosen words."

— Aanya Kapoor, Head Florist, Rose & Stem

Variety & Range

Our bouquet range spans twelve distinct collections — each curated around a mood, occasion, or colour palette. Whether you need something classic and timeless (pure red roses, white lilies) or contemporary and bold (tropical anthuriums, bird-of-paradise, deep plum dahlias), we have an arrangement built for it.

Tip: Order before 10 AM for the widest selection — our most popular varieties like Juliet Garden Roses and Sahara Ranunculus sell out early!

Sizes & Presentation

Every bouquet is available in three sizes — Petite (15–20 stems), Classic (25–35 stems), and Grand (45–60 stems). All are wrapped in our signature craft paper and available with optional add-ons like chocolates, greeting cards, or fragrant candles.

Flower Care Tips

  • Trim stems 2 cm at a 45° angle on arrival
  • Change water every 2 days with flower food
  • Keep away from direct sunlight & AC vents
  • Remove wilted leaves to prevent bacteria
  • Store overnight in a cool, dark spot
  • Mist petals lightly to extend freshness

Our Process

From Farm to Your Door in 5 Steps

We obsess over every detail so that your flowers arrive looking like they just bloomed.

1

Farm Harvest at Dawn

Our partner farms harvest blooms before 4 AM — when stems are fully hydrated and turgid. Flowers are immediately cold-stored at 2°C and transported within the same morning.

2

Quality Inspection

Every batch is inspected by our QC team — checking petal firmness, stem hydration, and colour vibrancy. Any stem that doesn't meet our standard is returned to the farm.

3

Florist Design & Arrangement

Your order is assigned to a specialist florist who matches blooms to your chosen style. They hand-arrange, condition stems, and balance the composition for maximum visual impact and longevity.

4

Premium Wrapping & Card

Each bouquet is wrapped in triple-layer craft paper, finished with a hand-tied satin ribbon, and paired with your personalised message card — written by hand or printed in elegant calligraphy.

5

Tracked Delivery to Door

Your bouquet travels in a climate-controlled delivery vehicle. You and the recipient receive real-time SMS and WhatsApp updates at every stage until the moment it arrives.
