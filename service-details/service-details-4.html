About This Service

Why Subscribe to Flowers?

There's something deeply calming about fresh flowers in a space. Studies show that having flowers at home reduces stress, boosts mood, and improves productivity. Our subscription service makes that feeling effortless — no more impulse trips to a florist or forgetting to refresh arrangements.

Every delivery is a surprise curated by our florists around the season, the month, and current farm availability. You'll never receive the same arrangement twice. Each box includes a care card, flower food sachet, and a seasonal note from your florist.

"Our subscribers say it best: the moment the flowers arrive, it feels like a gift — even when you bought them for yourself."

— Naina Joshi, Subscription Manager, Rose & Stem

Your Schedule, Your Way

Choose from weekly, fortnightly, or monthly delivery cycles. Select your preferred delivery day, building size preference (Petite, Classic, or Grand), and any flower preferences or allergies. You can update your preferences any time from your subscriber dashboard.

Subscriber Perks: Save up to 30%, free delivery on all cycles, first access to limited seasonal collections, priority customer support, and a complimentary free box on your 6-month anniversary.

Gifting a Subscription

A flower subscription makes one of the most thoughtful and lasting gifts. Choose a 3-month, 6-month, or 12-month gift subscription for a loved one — we'll send a beautiful gift certificate and handle every delivery directly to them, with a personalised note from you attached to each box.
