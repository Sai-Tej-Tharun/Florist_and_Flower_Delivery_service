About This Service

When Time Is Short, Love Shouldn't Wait

Life moves fast. Birthdays sneak up. Anniversaries get forgotten. That apology sometimes needs to happen today, not tomorrow. Our same-day delivery service exists precisely for these moments — so that no matter how last-minute, you can still make someone feel truly special.

Our fleet of temperature-controlled delivery vehicles is stationed across the city, ready to move the moment your order is confirmed. Our florists work in parallel — arranging your bouquet while the driver is already on route — so that from order to doorbell is as fast as humanly possible.

"We built same-day delivery for the person who realises at 10 AM that today is the day they need flowers."

— Rohan Das, Operations Head, Rose & Stem

How Fast Is Fast?

Orders placed before 12:00 PM are guaranteed delivery by 4:00 PM the same day. Orders placed before 3:00 PM are delivered by 7:00 PM. Express orders (placed before 11 AM with the express add-on) are delivered within 2 hours.

Cutoff times: Standard same-day — order by 12 PM. Extended window — order by 3 PM. Express 2-hour — order by 11 AM (₹149 extra). Sunday orders close at 11 AM.

Coverage Cities

We currently serve same-day delivery in Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kochi, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Kolkata and 15+ additional cities. Enter your pin code at checkout to confirm availability in your area.

How It Works

Order to Doorstep in 4 Steps

Our same-day delivery is engineered for speed without sacrificing an ounce of quality.

1

Place Your Order

Order online, via WhatsApp, or by phone. Select your bouquet, speed, and delivery address. Confirm payment.

2

Florist Starts Immediately

Within 10 minutes of confirmation, your order is assigned to a florist who begins arranging — using the freshest stems from that morning's stock.

3

Real-Time Tracking Begins

You receive a WhatsApp message with live updates: "Bouquet ready ✅", "Driver assigned 🚗", "En route 📍". No wondering, no waiting blindly.

4

Delivered & Confirmed

Our driver hands over the bouquet, takes a delivery photo for your records, and sends a confirmation. You're done — they're delighted.
