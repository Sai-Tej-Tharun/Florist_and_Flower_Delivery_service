Our Brand Story

Where Every Petal
Tells a Story

Rose & Stem began in 2016 when founder Priya Mehta couldn't find a florist who truly understood the emotion behind gifting flowers.

Armed with a passion for horticulture and a belief that every bouquet should feel deeply personal, she started arranging flowers from her Mumbai apartment — wrapping each one with a handwritten note.

Word spread. One bouquet became ten. Ten became a thousand. Today, Rose & Stem operates across 12 cities, with a team of 28 master florists, a same-day delivery network, and a subscription platform that helps thousands of people never miss life's most meaningful moments.

But the heart of it all remains unchanged: every bouquet we make is a love letter from one person to another.

Farm to Doorstep

From the Heart of the Farm

Our flowers travel from certified organic farms in Pune, Ooty, and the Nilgiris — arriving at your door within 24 hours of harvest.

We partner exclusively with farms that practise responsible growing — no harmful pesticides, fair labour standards, and water-efficient irrigation. Because beautiful flowers should come from beautiful places.

Certified Organic Farms

All partner farms hold organic certification and undergo regular quality audits.

Harvested Within 24 Hours

Blooms are cut, cold-stored, and dispatched within 24 hours to ensure peak freshness.

Fair Farmer Partnerships

We pay above fair-trade rates and invest in the communities that grow our flowers.

Our Journey

From One Bouquet to a Movement

Eight years of growth, love, and flowers — milestone by milestone.

2016

The Beginning

Priya Mehta starts arranging bouquets from her Mumbai apartment, delivering personally with handwritten notes.

2017

First Studio Opens

A small studio in Bandra becomes our first official workshop — 4 florists, 50 orders per week.
